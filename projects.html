Our Core Pillars

Our work is centered on three pillars that directly address the core challenges faced by indigenous Aeta communities in Tarlac, ensuring sustainable and culturally relevant development.

Quality Education

We provide scholarships, school supplies, and establish learning hubs to ensure every child has access to formal education and necessary resources.

View Related Projects

Digital Literacy

Bridging the digital divide by equipping youth with essential computer skills and internet access, preparing them for the modern economy.

View Related Projects

Community Support

Focusing on health, nutrition, and cultural preservation initiatives that empower the community while respecting their unique heritage.
