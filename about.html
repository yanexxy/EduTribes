Who We Are and Our Mission

Learn more about the heart of EduTribes, our history, and the values that drive our commitment to indigenous education.

Bridging the Gap in Indigenous Education

Founded in 2024, EduTribes is dedicated to the Aeta communities in Tarlac. We believe that geography and heritage should never be a barrier to quality education.

Our mission is to provide holistic support—from backpacks to building classrooms—ensuring that every child has the tools to build their own future. We collaborate closely with local community leaders to ensure our projects are sustainable and culturally appropriate.
